Mail Stock Store — Warranty & Replacement Policy

1. Warranty Period

All accounts include a 7-day replacement warranty from the date and time of delivery unless otherwise stated on the product page.

  • For new 0–3 days email → 1 day warranty
  • For new 4–7 days email → 7 days warranty

The warranty expires automatically after the stated period. Once expired, the claim button is disabled and shown as "Expired".

2. Warranty Coverage

You are eligible for a free replacement if:

  • The account credentials provided are incorrect.
  • The account cannot be accessed immediately after delivery.
  • The delivered account does not match the advertised specifications.
  • The account has an issue that existed before delivery and was not disclosed.
  • We mistakenly deliver the wrong account.

3. Warranty Does NOT Cover

The warranty becomes void if:

  • You changed the password.
  • You disabled 2-Step Verification.
  • You modified any security settings.
  • You shared the account with another person.
  • You logged in from multiple countries within a short period.
  • You used VPNs or proxies that triggered security checks.
  • You used automation tools, bots, scripts, or bulk email software.
  • You sent spam or violated the email provider's policies.
  • You deleted important account information.
  • You ignored security warnings.
  • You requested a replacement after the warranty period expired.
  • The issue was caused by your own actions.

4. Replacement Policy

If your claim is approved:

  • We will provide an equivalent replacement account.
  • Replacement accounts will have specifications equal to or better than the original purchase.
  • Refunds are generally not provided if a replacement is available.

5. Verification Process

Our support team will:

  • Review the submitted evidence.
  • Verify the account status.
  • Respond within 24–48 hours.

False claims may result in permanent suspension from our store.

6. Replacement Limit

  • Each purchased account is eligible for one replacement only.
  • The replacement account does not receive a new warranty period unless explicitly stated.

7. Buyer Responsibilities

The buyer is responsible for:

  • Safely storing login credentials.
  • (More important) Use Gmail (Mobile) to log in to email.
  • Following the setup instructions provided.
  • Using the account responsibly.
  • Keeping account information confidential.
  • Reporting problems immediately.

8. Abuse Prevention

We reserve the right to deny warranty claims if:

  • Fraud is suspected.
  • Edited screenshots are submitted.
  • False information is provided.
  • The buyer attempts to exploit the warranty system.
  • Multiple suspicious claims are submitted.
