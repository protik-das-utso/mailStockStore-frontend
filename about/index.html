About MailStock.store

MailStock.store is a curated marketplace for digital assets. Sellers submit inventory to be reviewed by our admin team. Once approved, items are listed for buyers to purchase — with warranty and no direct communication between parties.

How it works

  1. Sellers submit assets with an asking price.
  2. Admin reviews, approves or counters, then inventories the item.
  3. Buyers browse, purchase and receive instant delivery.
  4. Warranty claims are handled through our support team.
